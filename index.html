Intro

IN THERE facilitates Space, Ambience, Amenities, and Resources to help people indulge in the various forms of Art and Meditation.

AGENDA

IN THERE is an Open Art and Meditation Studio. Our activities here are not predefined. We do not teach, host competitions or arrange exhibitions and neither do we decide the purpose of your visit. Our Agenda is simple, Relaxation, Recreation, and Mental Peace.

Inthere

Aim

IN THERE believes that the best way to liberate Mental Health from the stigma, inhibitions, and taboos is to bring it out in the open!

We aim to make Mental Health Aid More Accessible, Approachable, and Affordable. Our one-step toward prioritizing Mental Fitness would etch directions for future generations.
